More on mutual aid.

This will sometimes refer to different group size
tiers:
- I: Up to 10
- II: Up to 30
- III: Up to 60
- IV: Up to 150
- V: Up to 500

# Kinds of help needed

*   Marriage pools:
    *   50 people required to prevent immediate
        inbreeding depression
    *   500 for long-term viability (although 150 OK
        according to some models)

*   There are generally missing required skills in
    smaller groups. High gaps at tier I, minimal at
    tier IV.
*   Food supply shocks and disasters can affect every
    tier, but are less of an issue as you go up.

*   Tier II+ needed to harvest
*   Tier III+ needed to build a full-size house or
    do major repairs (takes too long spinning out
    the labor between farming tasks)
*   Tier III+ needed to buffer against individual
    sickness or injury (major epidemics still a
    problem)
*   Tier III+ needed to have sufficient spare parts
    inventory

*   Trade goods required for specialized infrastructure
    and rituals

# Notes

*   Per capita help required can be negative exponential
    in group size
